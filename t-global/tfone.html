Some Main advantages of IPPBX:

  • Combines five services - Phone, Internet, Conferencing, Long Distance, and PBX into one manageable monthly bill.
  • No Onsite PBX
    • Eliminates Capital Expenditures and long-term leasing requirements of traditional PBX systems.
    • Adding multiple locations and remote employees is only a matter of adding a phone instead of costly traditional PBX capacity cards.
  • Easy on your overheads in the long run
    • Low upfront implementation cost
    • Cut international and long distance calling costs.
  • Better Reliability & Maintenance
    • Faults are detected & services recovered quickly
    • Seamless Maintenance – remote maintenance is made possible.
    • Moves/adds/changes- no hard wiring or adjusting of cables.
  • Flexible pricing. Because of the nature of VoIP and IP PBX you only choose the exact services and hardware you need. There’s no need to buy additional service or costly hardware.
